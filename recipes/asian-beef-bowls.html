Asian Beef Bowls

An easy, super fast weeknight dinner packed with flavour!

Best served with rice, the mince can be from whichever animal you prefer to sacrifice.

Ingredients

    Sauce:

  • 1 tbspn Hoisin sauce
  • 2 tbspn Oster sauce
  • 1 tbspn Chinese cooking wine
  • 2 1/2 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 2 tsp white sugar
  • 1/2 tsp sesame oil
  • 1/4 tsp white pepper

    • Stir fry:

  • 1 tbspn oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • Half a cabbage, diced roughly
  • 500g mince, beef (any kind is fine)

    • To serve:

  • 1/2 cup shallots/scallions, sliced
  • Sesame seeds
  • Cooked white rice
  • Side dish: Pickled Radish
  • Side dish: Smashed Cucumbers

Steps

  1. Mix sauce together in a bowl.
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add garlic, onion, and chopped cabbage, cook for 5 minutes until onions and cabbage are brown and cooked through.
  3. Add mince and cook, breaking it up as you go.
  4. Once the mince has browned, add sauce and one cup of water. Cook for a further 15 minutes or until sauce has evaporated and beef and cabbage is cooked and softened.
  5. Serve over rice and garnish with sliced shallots and sesame seeds.
