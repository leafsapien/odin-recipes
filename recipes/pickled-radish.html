Pickled Radish

Crunchy pickled radish (aka Mu-Pickle) that tastes just like your local banchan!

Make a big batch to store in your shed once you realise how it smells on day two.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/3 cup caster sugar
  • 1 1/2 cup white vinegar
  • 4 tbspn coarse sea salt
  • 3 cups water
  • 900g Korean radish, cut in to 7mm cubes

Steps

  1. Mix the sugar, vinegar, salt and water in a small bowl until the sugar and salt are completely dissolved.
  2. Place the radish in a glass jar and pour over the prepared liquid ensuring that the liquid covers the radish completely.
  3. Refrigerate for at least 24 hours before eating. Can be kept in the fridge for up to one month. Alternatively when the excessive sulphuric smell overtakes your fridge you can banish it to your garden shed to be forgotten.
